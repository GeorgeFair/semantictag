Why Are Glaciers melting?

Glaciers and sea ice are shrinking because the planet is warming: rising greenhouse gas emissions trap more heat, which drives air and ocean temperatures up, disrupts long-term climate patterns, and accelerates melt faster than winter snow and ice can rebuild.

What We Can Do

  • Cut fossil fuel use by choosing clean electricity, public transit, and efficient vehicles.
  • Improve home efficiency with better insulation, smart thermostats, and energy-smart appliances.
  • Support policies and companies that invest in renewable power and rapid emissions cuts.
  • Protect forests and wetlands that store carbon and buffer coasts from rising seas.
  • Stay informed and vote for leaders who act on climate science and climate justice.
